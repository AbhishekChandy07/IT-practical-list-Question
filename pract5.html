Football in India is a growing sport with a passionate fan base. Despite cricket's dominance, football is gaining popularity, fueled by domestic leagues like the Indian Super League (ISL) and I-League. The sport's grassroots development is supported by various initiatives, encouraging youth participation. India's national team competes globally, striving for improvement. Renowned players like Sunil Chhetri have contributed to raising the sport's profile. Challenges include infrastructure limitations and historical cricket-centric preferences. However, football's upward trajectory in India signals a promising future, with increased investment, grassroots efforts, and growing enthusiasm among fans.
